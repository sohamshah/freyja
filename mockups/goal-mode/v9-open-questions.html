Long stock + short OTM call. Capped upside at strike + premium; downside cushioned by premium but otherwise the stock's. — Investopedia, OptionsPlaybook

How are short-term vs long-term capital gains treated for covered-call writes?
Premium is short-term capital gain on expiry; assignment can convert long-term holdings to a short-term sale if the call is held into the qualified period. — IRS Pub 550
